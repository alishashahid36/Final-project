Mission Statement and Goals
Mission
Purpose
Provide an online platform for Rawalpindi and Islamabad to find and order custom floral bouquets.
Aim
Making the bouquet ordering process simple and inspiring moments of happiness and celebrations.
Project Motivation
The website aims to extend the digital presence of 'Florative Decor', a home-based floral decor business in Rawalpindi and Islamabad. The project seeks to reach a wider audience and enable them to explore, appreciate, and purchase custom floral arrangements.
Thinking Theory
1. Idea Showcase Florative Decor's unique floral designs. 2. Target Audience People in Rawalpindi and Islamabad seeking exclusive floral arrangements. 3. What Introduce Floralative Decor's unique designs to a wider audience. 4. Where Distinguish Florative Decor with personalized designs. 5. When Launch post-completion for effective customer needs fulfilment. 6. Why Enhance user experience with intuitive browsing and ordering tools. 7. How Feature a clickable gallery of flower bouquets for details. 8. Unique Proposition Start with the unique idea of Florative Decor.
Target Audience
The primary aim is to target individuals in Rawalpindi and Islamabad, aged approximately 15 and above, interested in unique floral designs for various occasions like weddings, events, home decor, and personal gifting.
Mind Map
Website Content Structure
Features
Featured arrangements, Fresh flowers, Flower and bouquet colors, and Quantity options.
Customer Engagement
Welcome message, Reviews, Testimonials, and Mission and values.
Interaction
Ribbon options, Payment options, Customization options, Product catalog.
Logistics
Pick-up information, Event orders, Delivery information, Order tracking, Contact details, and Social media links.
